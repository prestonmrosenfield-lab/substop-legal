## 1. Introduction

Atlas Budgeting ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Atlas Budgeting mobile application and related services (collectively, the "App").

Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the App.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- **Account information:** Email address and password when you create an account.
- **Subscription data:** Names, amounts, billing dates, and categories of subscriptions you add manually or that are detected automatically.
- **Payment preferences:** Your budget limits and notification preferences (stored locally on your device and in our secure database).

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Email metadata (Gmail only, with your explicit permission):** When you connect your Gmail account, we scan only emails from known subscription senders (e.g., Netflix, Spotify, Amazon) to detect subscription receipts, trial expiration notices, and price change alerts. **We never read personal emails. We never store the content of your emails.** We extract only: sender name, subscription service name, amount, and billing date.
- **Bank transaction data (with your explicit permission):** If you connect your bank via Plaid, we receive read-only access to transaction descriptions and amounts to detect recurring subscription charges. We do not receive your full account numbers, routing numbers, or the ability to move money.
- **Usage data:** App opens, feature interactions, and crash reports to help us improve the product. This data is anonymized and aggregated.
- **Device information:** Device type, operating system version, and app version for troubleshooting purposes.

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

- **Google (Gmail OAuth):** We receive your Gmail email address and read-only access to emails from subscription senders you authorize. This access can be revoked at any time at [myaccount.google.com/permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions).
- **Plaid (bank connection):** We receive tokenized access to your transaction history. Plaid's privacy policy applies to their data handling: [plaid.com/legal/privacy-notice](https://plaid.com/legal/privacy-notice).
- **RevenueCat (purchases):** We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. They receive your App Store/Google Play purchase receipts. RevenueCat's privacy policy: [revenuecat.com/privacy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy).

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Detect and display your active subscriptions, trials, and upcoming charges
- Send push notifications for upcoming charges, trial expirations, price changes, and zombie subscriptions (with your explicit permission)
- Generate personalized money-saving recommendations
- Provide weekly spending digests and analytics
- Respond to your support requests
- Improve and debug the App
- Comply with legal obligations

We do **not**:

- Sell your personal data to third parties
- Read, store, or share your personal emails
- Use your financial data for advertising purposes
- Share your subscription list with other users

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL), hosted on AWS infrastructure with SOC 2 compliance.
- All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256).
- Gmail and bank access tokens are stored encrypted and are never logged or exposed.
- We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time (see Section 8).

---

## 5. Data Sharing

We share your information only with:

- **Service providers** who help us operate the App (Supabase for database, Sentry for crash reporting, RevenueCat for purchases, Plaid for bank connections). These providers are contractually prohibited from using your data for any purpose other than providing services to us.
- **Law enforcement or legal process** when required by law or to protect our rights.

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

Atlas Budgeting is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at the address below.

---

## 7. Your Choices and Controls

- **Notifications:** You can enable or disable specific notification types within the App at any time (Settings → Notifications).
- **Gmail access:** You can revoke our access to your Gmail at any time at [myaccount.google.com/permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions).
- **Bank connection:** You can disconnect your bank account within the App (Settings → Connected Accounts).
- **Account deletion:** You can delete your account and all associated data within the App (Settings → Delete Account) or by contacting us at the address below. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

---

## 8. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account exists. Upon account deletion:

- Your subscription data, preferences, and account information are permanently deleted within 30 days.
- Anonymized, aggregated usage statistics (with no personal identifiers) may be retained.
- We may retain records required for legal compliance for up to 7 years.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Atlas Budgeting is operated from the United States. If you are located outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States. We take appropriate safeguards to ensure your data is handled in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
